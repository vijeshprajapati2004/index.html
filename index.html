Top Places

Bhadrakali Temple

Bhadrakali Temple is one of the oldest temples for Goddess Bhadrakali.

Warangal Fort

Warangal Fort is located in Warangal District, It was the capital city of Kakatiya dynasty and the Musunuri Nayakas.

Ramappa Temple

Ramappa Temple, also known as the Rudreswara temple, is a Kakatiya style Hindu temple dedicated to the Hindu god Shiva.

Bhadrakali Lake

Bhadrakali Lake is a lake in Warangal, Telangana built by Ganapati of Kakatiya dynasty.

Laknavaram Lake

Laknavaram Lake is a lake in Govindaraopet mandal in Mulugu district, Warangal

Padmakshi Temple

Padmakshi Temple is one of the oldest temples. It is dedicated to the Hindu goddess Padmakshi

Thousand Pillar Temple

The Thousand Pillar Temple is a historical Hindu temple located in the town of Hanamakonda,

Bogatha Waterfall

Bogatha Waterfall is a waterfall located in Wazeedu Mandal 40Km from Warangal.
